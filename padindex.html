Inspiration

These are some of the photos Pinterest curated that is similar to Paddington Markets. From these photos, we can select complementary colours for the website. The complete moodboard can be found here.

Wireframe

This wireframe illustrates how the redesigned homepage will look like. For the new site, the structure is more uniform whereas the old site, content and links were seemingly randomly placed on the page.

The old site has a tiny short video embedded to the side of the page. This design is similar to random video ads that pop up when the user is looking at the main content. For the redeisgned site, the video is enlarged and placed at the centre of the page. This tells the user that this video is intended for the website and it's a video about Paddington Markets instead of a random ad to the side of the page.

Typography

For the redesigned page, 2 fonts were chosen, Megrim and Raleway. These fonts are readily available from Google Fonts. These fonts were chosen because it is attractive to the target market that would visit this site, as well as reflect the type and style of products advertised on the page. The primary target market are hipsters and people who like hand-made products.

Megrim will be used for the nav bar and large headings, and Raleway will be used for regular text. Both fonts are web-friendly.

Colours

These colours were chosen because it attacts the primary target market and reflects the style and tastes of the vendors and visitors.
